    QUALITY & SERVICE:


    While quality is our hallmark, service is our pride. When you step into our outlets you will surely observe how we have painstakingly put processes in place to make your visit worthwhile and comfortable.     Since we have a wide variety of confectionery products, hygiene takes the highest priority for our entire workforce. Our food handlers have been trained to follow sound practices to ensure that anything that we serve is safe for consumption. To ensure that, we stipulate that they wear head caps and gloves.
